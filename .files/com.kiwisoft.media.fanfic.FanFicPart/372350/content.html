Mr. Corelli stood up from the chair and pushed it in. "That's what I like to hear, Miss Stewart. Now go on home… and rest."

"Thank you, Mr. Corelli." Miley's voice was soft, but she knew he heard her as a soft smile spread across his face. "I will see you on Monday."
