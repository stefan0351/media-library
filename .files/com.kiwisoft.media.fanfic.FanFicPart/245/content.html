"It's a work of art. Emeril couldn't do better."

"You've got some on your -"

Then she felt it. A little whipped cream had missed its mark, and ended up on the corner of her mouth. But she didn't move to lick it off. "Oh."

"No, I'll get it."

Buffy wasn't quite sure why she said that, because she knew Willow was perfectly capable, but she wanted to. She reached her finger out, and gathered the rebellious whipped cream on her fingertip. She saw Willow's eyes close briefly.

Then, she quickly licked the whipped cream off her finger. Her friend whimpered.

***

Willow walked over by the front door of the house and looked out the window while Buffy was putting the movie in the VCR. It was the full moon. A lot of things got thrown out of whack when the full moon was up. And emotions were at the top of the list. What had happened in the kitchen?

Something, that was for sure. Definitely something. The first thought that crossed her mind, after the thoughts of the possible outcomes for what could have happened in the kitchen, was it was a desert fluke. Xander was a fluke. Buffy was.

"No?"

"Topping hog!"

Both girls started to crack up.
