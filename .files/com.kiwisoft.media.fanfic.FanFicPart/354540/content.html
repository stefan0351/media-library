Buffy stood up and took Willows hand, "Care for one last dance before we head out on patrol?"

"Definitely," replied Willow.

Faith leaned over to Tara, "What ya say we join em?"

Before Tara could respond a mysterious cold breeze blew through the bronze, sending Buffy and Faiths slayer senses into over drive.

Tara wrapped her arms around the brunette slayer who had began trembling, "Faith, what's wrong?"

"He's here."

"Who's here?"

"The Warlock," replied Faith recalling the coldness she had felt during the other two encounters with the warlock.

"If he's here he's not alone," shouted Buffy as several vampires made their way into the bronze.

The two slayers and Angel took up fighting positions in front of the others as the vampires slowly began to make their way towards them. The stopped about twenty feet from the group and stood there glaring at the two slayer and their friends, the space in front of the vamps began to waiver then emit a deep red glow, followed by a bright flash that produced The Warlock.

Ciarán looked Buffy dead in the eyes, "Now I end this," snapped The Warlock, motioning the vampires to attack.

Tara began chanting a protection spell for the groups as Willow launched a rather large fireball towards the vampires only to have it dissipated by The Warlock.

"It's not going to be that easy to win this time Mage," shouted The Warlock. He raised his hand into the air and shot a bolt of lighting towards Buffy.

Willow quickly raised a magical shield around her beloved causing the bolt to reflect back at The Warlock, sending him flying into the wall.

The vampires charged them, focusing primarily on Faith and Angel. Buffy rushed over towards her fellow slayer and grabbed one of the attacking vampires by the back of the collar and flung him across the room. She proceeded to grab another vamp by the chin and decapitated him with her bare hands.

The Warlock stood up and launched a fireball towards Tara as she continued her protection chant, Willow focused on the fireball as it careened towards the blonde immortal and caused it to dissipate seconds before it would have struck her. She then turned her attention back to The Warlock only to see a second fireball strike the in the heart of the vampires who where attacking Angel. She stood there in horror as the vampires along with Angel where reduced to ashes.

The Warlock then shot another fireball towards Faith and Buffy who had just finished off the last of the vampires. He watched as the fireball sailed through the air and struck an invisible barrier that Willow had put between him and her friends.

"Well done young Mage, unfortunately for you I have more than one trick up my sleeve," replied the smirking Warlock as he vanished before her eyes.

Cordelia knelt down and ran her hand through the ashes where Angel had been fighting, tears started to stream down her cheeks as she though of her friend and mentor and all that he had done for her. She looked up at her friends who had formed a circle around her and sobbed, "He's gone."

"And good riddance, there's nothing worse than evil gone good," snapped Ciarán, reappearing next to Tara.

The warlock reached out with his right arm and shot several bolts of energy into Xander and Cordelia dropping both of them to the ground.

Ciarán looked at a stunned Willow, "It seems you have much to learn," he then turned towards Faith and looked her dead in the eye, "Since I couldn't get you to leave I guess I'll just have to take something you care about," Replied The Warlock as he grabbed Tara and vanished.
