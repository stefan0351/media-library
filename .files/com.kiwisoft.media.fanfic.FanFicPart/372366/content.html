Lilly laughed before continuing, "Yes, and I got to know a whole other side to you, one I've never seen as just your best friend… you were so fun and honest and even when you probably wanted to do anything else, you were open about everything. Miley nodded eagerly. "Hell yes. I've been waiting for it all night."

"Okay, then." Lilly relented, "Go ahead, open them."

Miley dropped her hands to her lap and revealed bright blue-grey eyes that clouded in confusion as she turned her body to face her friend. "Um, Lil… what are you giving me?" she asked, noting the lack of anything that looked even remotely close to a present.

"I'm giving you the real Lilly Truscott."
