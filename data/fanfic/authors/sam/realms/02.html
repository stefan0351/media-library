"Sorry to disappoint you," quipped Buffy.

"Don't worry about it, I plan on changing that circumstance right now," growled the warlock as he raised his hands into the air and shot a bolt of energy right at Buffy.

The bolt soared directly towards here but immediately dissipated when it hit the protective field that Willow and Tara had through up over the slayers.

"Somebody's been practicing, try and stop this."

"Sure thing Red."

"Your wish is my command," replied Buffy as she gave Willow a quick peck on the cheek for good luck before springing into action.

Buffy rushed the warlock from the right and Faith from the left. He waved his hands through the air and sent Buffy falling to the ground, only to be blind sided by Faith.

As the Slayers fought the warlock Willow and Tara quickly drew a circle outline on the ground and sat down inside of it, they then placed the Banishing Crystal in the center of the circle and began the chant Fey had taught them a few hours ago.

Ciarán spun around in rage and lifted his right hand in the air. It began to glow with magical energy, "See if that puny protective field can withstand this."

Before the warlock could cast his spell Buffy swept his legs out from under him. He fell flat on his back and the energy blast that was ment for Faith shot up straight into the air and blew a large hole in the warehouse ceiling sending rubble falling down around them.

Ciarán vanished before either one of the slayers could get in another punch. He re-appeared several feet away from them, "And this is for thinking you could beat me."

Buffy and Faith quickly jumped to their feet and took a defensive stance.

The warlock stared at them for a moment then raised both his hands towards the ceiling,. A large ball of fire formed above his head, "Now I end this."

"Buffy, Faith...NOW," Shouted Tara causing the two slayers to dive towards the wall of the warehouse.

A bright beam of blue light shot from the banishing stone and struck the warlock in the chest lifting him up off of the ground and canceling out his spell.

He screamed in agony as the gems power opened up a porthole to Limbo around him. The two slayers picked themselves off of the floor and watched in astonishment as the field of light grew brighter and brighter, then simply vanished into nothingness along with the warlock
