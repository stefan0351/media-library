"What do you want?"

Finally getting that unpleasant business out of the way, I walked towards the mall where Willow had planned some movie thing for all of us to go to so we weren't laying around the house ... again. I hoped this time, I could talk Buffy into getting some nachos and cheese. I couldn't believe her the first time we'd gone and she absolutely refused to buy it cause she didn't want to kiss me with nacho breath.

Like I'd mind.
