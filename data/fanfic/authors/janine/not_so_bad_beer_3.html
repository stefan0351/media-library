"Don't you just hate those lions," Buffy responded.

"With a passion," Willow answered.

They remained in silence after that for a moment.

"Will?" Buffy asked breaking the silence. "I didn't do anything last night did I? You know, anything that could possibly get me arrested or anything? Did I?"
